Tenant Introduction Process

Ensuring a secure and smooth transition for tenants.

Updated Tenant Introduction Process (March 3, 2025)

Dear Property Owners and Brokers,

Please follow the revised tenant introduction process at Alexa Co-Operative Housing Society Limited:

  1. The registered property owner must email Tenant.Alexa@gmail.com from their society-registered email ID using this format.
  2. The society will review the request and provide further instructions, including a list of required documents.
  3. Once submitted, the documents will be reviewed. Any additional information or clarifications will be requested if needed.
  4. Upon successful document validation, a mandatory one-on-one induction meeting will be scheduled with the Managing Committee. Only tenants are required to attend—owners are not required, and brokers will not be permitted.
  5. Failure to submit the required documents or attend the induction meeting may result in a delay or denial of approval.

Important Notes

The document verification process takes approximately 10 days. Please avoid rushing the process.
