1. General Conduct

  • Arrive on time and be in full uniform with identity card visible at all times.
  • Maintain a polite, professional, and helpful manner with residents, visitors, and staff.
  • Refrain from using mobile phones for personal use during duty hours.

2. Access Control

  • Verify identity of all visitors, delivery personnel, and service providers before allowing entry.
  • Maintain an entry/exit register for all visitors and contractors; use MyGate and wait for approval before releasing them
  • Do not permit unauthorized vehicles or persons inside the premises.

3. Patrolling & Surveillance

  • Conduct regular patrols of the entire property (Including all 35 floors of both Wing, Terrace, Common Area, Amenities Area, Underground Water Plant, Sewrage Plant etc), extreme focus on all entry/exit points.
  • Check that all gates, doors, and vulnerable points are locked as per shift schedule.

4. Emergency Procedures

  • Familiarize yourself with fire exits, evacuation plans, and location of fire-fighting equipment.
  • Report any fire or safety hazards immediately to the management committee and act as per emergency protocols.
  • In case of medical emergencies, promptly assist and inform the nearest hospital/ambulance service and management.

5. Parking & Traffic Control

  • Allow only authorized vehicles into the premised only upto the starting of Kids Play area. (Check for parking stickers/passes, residents identity)
  • Always give priority to ambulances, wheelchairs, or disabled persons.
  • Prevent parking in no-parking zones and keep fire / emergency lanes clear at all times.

6. Communication & Reporting

  • Maintain clear communication with fellow staff and the management committee via group/intercom/phone.
  • Report any incidents, violations, or maintenance issues in the daily logbook to Society Committee Chairman and Secretary
  • Do not share internal information about residents or operations with outsiders.

7. Amenities Safety

  • Ensure amenities like Kids Play Area, Todler Area, Indoor Play Area, Gym etc., are used within allowed hours and safety rules are followed.
  • Report any accidents or misuse of amenities immediately.

Shift Schedule

  • Kids Play Area - Operational 24 Hours
  • Toddler Play Area - Operational from 5 AM to 10 PM all 7 days
  • Indoor Play Area - Operational from 5 AM to 10 PM all 7 days
